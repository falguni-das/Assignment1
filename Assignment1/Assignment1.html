Introduction: In an increasingly digital world, the role of technology in empowering marginalized groups cannot be overstated. Under this present reality, to uplift women across the nation Her Power Project: Women Empowerment through ICT Frontier Initiative is a groundbreaking initiative taken by the Bangladesh Government. This initiative not only addresses the gender digital divide but also creates pathways for women to access education, economic opportunities, and thereby fostering gender equality and social progress.
